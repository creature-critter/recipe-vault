ingredient notes

while you can use whatever brand ingredients you have, i like to take note of what i use on a regular basis for consistency sake! the ingredients listed below will be what i use across recipes unless otherwise stated :3

  • flour
    • king arthur brand flours - bread, ap, and whole wheat
  • sugar
    • kirkland brand organic sugar
    • kirkland brand organic brown sugar
  • yeast
    • fleischmann's active dry yeast
  • salt
    • kirkland brand sea salt
    • morton kosher salt
  • chocolate
    • 72% cacao dark chocolate bits - sometimes i use 85-90%
    • guittard cocoa rouge powder
