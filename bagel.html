plain bagel

makes 7 bagels

ingredients

  • 9 grams active dry yeast
  • 29 grams granulated sugar
  • 345 grams lukewarm water
  • 660 grams ap flour
  • 9 grams salt
  • 2 tablespoons honey for pre-baking boil

tools

small bowl

large bowl

kitchen scale

large pot

slotted spoon

baking sheet

parchment paper

time to make some bagels!

step 1 - knead & proof dough

  1. in small bowl combine sugar, lukewarm water, and yeast. let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. in large bowl weigh out flour and salt.
  3. add wet to dry in large bowl. mix until flour is just absorbed.
  4. move dough from large bowl onto lightly floured surface and knead for 10-15 minutes, or until dough is smooth and elastic.
  5. after kneading, move dough to lightly oiled large bowl and cover. cold proof in fridge overnight, or up to 14 hours.

step 2 - shape dough

  1. after cold proof, place dough on counter to warm up for 30 minutes to an hour.
  2. once warm, cut dough at 155 grams. keep dough pieces covered. you should have about 7 pieces at this weight, but feel free to adjust to your liking.
  3. shape dough pieces into balls. keep dough balls covered.
  4. with the dough ball in your hand, pierce the center of the ball with your finger. stretch and shape the ball into a bagel shape. make the center of the bagle ~4 inches wide. the hole should be much larger than you expect, the dough will retract while it rests and once it's boiled!
  5. once all bagels are shaped, let rest for 15 minutes.

step 3 - boil & bake dough

  1. while bagels are resting, set oven to preheat @ 425F
  2. fill pot with enough water to cover bagels, add the honey, cover pot, set water to boil.
  3. after resting, place bagels in boiling water for 1 minute, flip and boil for another minute on the other side. remove from water and place on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. once all bagels are boiled, place in oven for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown
